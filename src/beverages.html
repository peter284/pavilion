Yenda Pale Ale
Yenda Pale Ale is an Australian-style pale ale with pronounced floral and tropical fruit hop aromas and subtle malt character. The bitterness from the New World hops balances the full-bodied malt flavour, delivering a refreshing, clean finish.
Yenda Golden Ale
Australian Beer Company Yenda Golden Ale is light amber in appearance with malty flavours and is balanced with a clean hoppiness. The aroma is strong on passion and citrus with a zesty crisp finish. Goes well with salty snacks, white meats and fish.
Yenda Crisp
Yenda is all about keeping it local. The barley used in the Yenda range of beers is sourced from within 40kms of the brewery, supporting the local farmers of the Riverina. Crisp Lager has a light smooth body while retaining an intense but refined hop aroma with a crisp finish as the name suggests.
