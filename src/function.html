WEDDING CELEBRATIONS

Here at Suttons Beach we are passionate about making your day memorable. Our dedicated and friendly team are on hand to make sure your wedding day runs seamlessly, so you can sit back and enjoy your day with family and friends. Our two function rooms seat between 60 & 300 people and have breathtaking views of Moreton Bay showcasing beautiful sunsets over the water, making it the perfect venue for you and your guests. Our extensive facilities allow for both your ceremony and reception to be held at one convenient location with beautiful backdrops for your wedding photos. We pride ourselves in being able to tailor wedding packages to suit your personal style. Our professional functions team can cater for all types of weddings and budgets whether you want a relaxed cocktail styled wedding or an intimate sit down meal our chefs can create a menu focusing on using local seasonal produce accompanied with Australian and International beverages. Given out ideal location and affordability Suttons Beach Pavilion is understandably in demand. Book your free viewing with us today and let us help you make your wedding day one to remember

CONFERENCES & CORPORATE FUNCTIONS
Suttons Beach Pavilion offers numerous function spaces to hold your next corporate event. Whether this be a small gathering in our intimate boardroom, or a larger group in one of our two function rooms which individually can accommodate up to 150 delegates, or combined to accommodate 300 comfortably. Together with our professional food and beverage staff, our events co-ordinator can tailor a package and cater to your specific requirements and ensure your function runs smoothly.
