Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner or Drinks with uninterrupted views across Moreton Bay

Situated right on the beachfront at Suttons Beach, Redcliffe - with uninterrupted sweeping views across Moreton Bay.
Once you enter Suttons Pavilion, the first thing you notice is the beach view, which offers an overwhelming background to the clean line of the restaurant and café.
The venue is the ideal venue for your event, whether it be a Wedding, Birthday, Corporate event, or just a catch up with friends and family.

The proximity of the beach is a constant reminder of the link between a beach pavilion and the reinvented restaurant. While the historic landmark has now clearly outlived its original purpose, a new life has been breathed into its fabric with the new owners recently taking over the reins.

It is such a unique experience to sit in the Tapas Terrace Bar, Café or Restaurant and absorb the view and the activities of the beach and the bay. with its ever-present beach-goers and sailboats in the distance, or just grab some take away and sit on the beach or grass, or at one the many available tables.
