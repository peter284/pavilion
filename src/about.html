DEDICATED TO THEIR CRAFT

Our team of talented, hardworking chefs bring a wealth of experience and expertise to the the peninsular, with backgrounds working in many countries they ensure our regularly changing menus use the best of local, seasonal and sustainable ingredients to offer our customers unique tastes, textures and flavours.

The kitchen brigade

is lead by Head Chef Michael Harris, a two-hatted chef, Michael has been a prominent figure in the hospitality industry for over 20 years. Born and raised in the village of Bampton, Oxfordshire in the UK. Michael’s experience is heavily focused on gastronomy and fine dining, having started his apprenticeship at the flagship London hotel, the Dorchester. He then went on to train with the famous Andoni Luis Aduriz of Mugaritz and Philippe Legendre of La Cinq, both two-Michelin starred chefs. Coming from an impressive background, having worked with Jamie Oliver and Heston Blumenthal in highly esteemed kitchens across Europe and Asia. Michael arrived in Australia several years ago after a stint at Raffles Hotel Singapore and sometime in New Zealand and brings with him a wealth of knowledge, experience and passion to the Peninsular.
